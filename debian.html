Debian

The Grandaddy of them all.

See the family tree here

Debian is one of the oldest and longest-running Linux distributions. It was founded on August 16,1993 by Ian Murdock. The name Debian comes from a combination of Ian and his wife's name, Debra

Why Choose Debian?

Choose Debian if your biggest concern is stability in the strictest sense of the word. Debian only has major changes every two years, and just security updates in between.

Reasons to Avoid

Avoid Debian if you are:

  • A Gamer
  • Always chasing that shiny new thing
  • Buy/own new hardware

Backports do exist, but at that point you would be better off using a distro with a faster update cadence.

Tip: Debian stable is ideal for servers.
