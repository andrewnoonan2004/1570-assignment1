Ubuntu

The N00Bs choice

Comparing it to Debian, it is Peyton Manning to Archie Manning(Who even is that guy?). If you know of any linux distros, Ubuntu is almost certainly one of them.

view an (outdated) family tree for Ubuntu!

Why Choose Ubuntu?

Ubuntu is the best choice for you if you value ease of use. It is the most popular, so it is easy to find support online for it. The LTS has the most commercial software availability of any linux distro. It is generally well-tested, stable, and has been around for a long time.

Reasons to Avoid

  • You hate Snaps
  • You like to customize a lot
  • You prefer rolling releases
  • You want to be different

Note: Ubuntu has LTS and interim releases — choose accordingly.
